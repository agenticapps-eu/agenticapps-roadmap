    - `applyBackfillOutcome(state, projectId, outcome)`: PURE reducer over the `Map` — on "start" sets `{ pendingBackfill: true, planAheadOverride: false }` (optimistic in-sync); on "success" sets `{ pendingBackfill: false, planAheadOverride: false }`; on "failure"|"cancelled" sets `{ pendingBackfill: false, planAheadOverride: undefined }` (revert to real planAhead) and the caller records an error.
